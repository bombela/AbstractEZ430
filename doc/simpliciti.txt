Packet

addr              | 

0xFF -> broadcast
0x00 -> broadcast

status
	- 0xFF -> empty packet (probably useless).
	- 0000 0000 -> two 4bit values
	   btn info
	   
	- xxxx 0001 -> packet with position (x, y, z) 

	- 0001 xxxx -> Up left
	- 0010 xxxx -> Down left
	- 0011 xxxx -> Up right
